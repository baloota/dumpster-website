InShot Video Editor

100 Million people use InShot as their go-to video editor. It’s also used by many TikTok influencers and creators. They claim they’re the best free HD video editor and video maker – and they’re definitely right! Packed with all the right features, you can trim & cut your TikToks, blur backgrounds, add music, video effects, and so much more. 

Here are a few of their features:

  • Trim and cut video 
  • Split videos into two parts
  • Export in HD
  • Music, sound effects, and recorder
  • Texts & stickers
  • Video speed control
  • Video cropper
  • Photo editor
  • And so much more

Timbre

Timbre is a critically acclaimed app for editing audio and video files. It’s amazingly simple, easy to use, and is a high-quality TikTok video editing app that includes all of the essentials you didn’t know you needed. Not to mention, it’s completely free.  

If you’re looking for a basic, light-weight tool to use to edit TikTok videos, we recommend Timbre. It’s most popular features are: 

  • Video & audio cutter  
  • Video & audio joiner
  • Video & audio converter 
  • Video to audio 
  • Video to GIF 

PowerDirector 

PowerDirector is an Editor’s Choice Android app, so you know it’s worth the install just to try! With PowerDirector you can make stunning & engaging TikToks seamlessly. The app boasts one of the most comprehensive feature lists when it comes to video editing tools. 

It’s arguably one of the most professional TikTok editing apps, so if you’re looking for the best of the best, PowerDirector is probably your best choice. Here’s what it’s known for:

  • 4K editing & exporting 
  • Fast-forward or slow-motion videos with speed adjustment
  • Shaky cam footage fixer with video stabilizer
  • Green screen background replacements
  • Double exposure effects from video overlays and blending-modes
  • Trim, splice and rotate videos with simple taps
  • Control brightness, color and saturation with precision
  • Apply jaw-dropping effects and transitions with drag & drop

Vizmato

Vizmato is one of the most powerful video editing apps available on the Google Play Store! You can get all of your video editing needs out of it and then some! 

Users can clip, trim and edit multiple videos to create highly-engaging TikTok videos. And can add text, filters, themes, visual effects and background music to transform your video. Here are a few of it’s other highlighted features:

  • Slideshow maker 
  • Video effects 
  • Filters & themes
  • Video & GIF recorder
  • Free background music  
  • Gif maker with text
  • Fun audio FX  

YouCut

Sick of watermarks or paying for key features? YouCut is the video editing app for you. YouCut is a fantastic all-in-one stop shop for all of your TikTok content needs. It includes most, if not, all of the pro editing tools you need that other apps would charge you for. 

Here are the app’s main features:

  • 100% movie & video maker
  • Video merger 
  • Video trimmer & video cutter
  • Video splitter & video slicer
  • Video speed control
  • Photo slideshow maker
  • No watermarks
  • No ads when editing videos
  • Add music to video

FilmoraGO

FilmoraGO is a go-to video & photo editing tool loved by many! It gets the job done – nothing more nothing less! If you’re curious about what’s included in the free version here’s the list:

  • Import photo and video clips
  • Add music from FilmoraGo’s library of licensed songs
  • Use music from your own device
  • Play in reverse
  • Slow down & speed up
  • Transitions
  • Text & titles
