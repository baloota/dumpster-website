]]> To delete a file, simply tap the file, and press delete.

Files that are stored in Dumpster will remain there for as long as you have the app installed. If you’d like, you can also change your device settings to auto-clean after a predesignated amount of time.

To do so, tap on the button located in the upper corner of the app’s homepage. Select Auto Clean.

Choose how long you would like Dumpster to save your deleted files for, before permanently deleting them from your device:

  • 1 week
  • 1 month
  • 3 months

]]> As soon as your files are deleted, they will automatically appear on Dumpster’s homepage.

If you deleted a file and it doesn’t appear in Dumpster, try the following troubleshooting tips:

  1. Ensure the file format is supported by Dumpster by reviewing here
  2. Exclude Dumpster from any task killer, battery saver, or memory cleaner app settings. Examples:
    • Clean Master
    • DU Battery Saver
    • AVG Cleaner
    • Battery Doctor 
    • CCleaner

If you still can’t locate your file, send us a bug report from within the app, so that we can help locate the source of your specific problem.
