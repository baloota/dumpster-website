]]> Dumpster takes up the same amount of storage space as the files it contains. The more files you have, the less storage you’ll have. We recommend sorting & deleting unnecessary files every other month to save space. 

You can also use X-Cleaner, a free junk cleaning app to do the dirty work for you. 

If you need a lot more space, consider upgrading your account to store files on Dumpster Cloud. You’ll save phone storage and will be able to access photos, videos, and other files on desktop or other mobile devices.
