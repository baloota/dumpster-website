]]> Dumpster is able to store almost all forms of file types. The entire list can be found here. 

Dumpster stores:

  • Most photo, video, & audio files and documents 
  • Deleted apps and previous versions of apps 

Dumpster does not store:

  • Whatsapp, Telegram, or any other messaging app contents
  • Phone or device contacts
